# Vault

Vault is the Bear Family password manager, powered by Vaultwarden — a self-hosted Bitwarden-compatible server.

## Accessing Vault

Visit [vault.bearfamily.net](https://vault.bearfamily.net) in your browser or use any Bitwarden-compatible client.

## Installing the Browser Extension

1. Go to the extension store for your browser:
   - [Chrome / Edge](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/bitwarden/nngceckbapebfimnlniiiahkandclblb)
   - [Firefox](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/bitwarden-password-manager/)
2. Install the **Bitwarden** extension.
3. Open the extension and click **Settings** (gear icon).
4. Set **Server URL** to `https://vault.bearfamily.net`.
5. Click **Save** and log in with your Bear Family account.

## Installing on Mobile

1. Download **Bitwarden** from the App Store or Google Play.
2. Tap the region selector and choose **Self-hosted**.
3. Enter `https://vault.bearfamily.net` as the server URL.
4. Log in with your Bear Family account.

## Creating an Account

Contact the server admin to have an account created for you. Self-registration may be disabled.

## Tips

- Use the browser extension to auto-fill passwords on websites.
- Enable **biometric unlock** on mobile for quicker access.
- Organize your passwords into **Collections** to share them with family members.
